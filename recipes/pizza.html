🍕 Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. Baked in a hot oven, it has a crispy crust and a gooey, melty topping. It's endlessly customizable and perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (preferably San Marzano)
  • 200g (7 oz) fresh mozzarella cheese(sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until smooth and elastic (about 8–10 minutes). Cover and let rise for 1–1.5 hours until doubled in size.

  2. Preheat Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or higher if possible. If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.

  3. Shape the Dough:

    Punch down the dough and divide it in half for two pizzas. Roll or stretch one portion into a round pizza base on a floured surface.

  4. Add Toppings:

    Spread tomato sauce over the dough, add mozzarella slices, and a few basil leaves. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.

  5. Bake:

    Transfer to the hot oven or stone. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

  6. Serve:

    Garnish with more basil if desired and serve hot.
