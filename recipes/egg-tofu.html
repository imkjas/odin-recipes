Description

This egg and tofu dish is a comforting, protein-packed meal that brings together soft tofu and gently cooked eggs in a savory soy-based sauce. Inspired by simple home-style cooking, it uses pantry staples like soy sauce, a touch of sugar, and a crumbled Knorr chicken cube for deep umami flavor. Lightly pan-fried tofu gives the dish a bit of texture, while the eggs add a silky, rich finish. Served over rice, itâ€™s a quick, satisfying option for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cube Knorr chicken cube(crumble)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Knorr base

    Dissolve the crumbled Knorr cube in hot water. Add soy sauce and sugar. Mix until fully combined

  2. Prepare the Egg:

    Stirr 2 eggs until smooth.

  3. Cook the tofu and egg:

    2 minutes pan-fry the tofu, then drizzle the egg on top.

  4. Combine Tofu, egg and sauce:

    Add the sauce to the pan and mix it slightly.

    5. Finish and Serve:

    On top of rice, add the egg and tofu dish.

Calories

  • 1 pack of tofu (300g) - 240kcal
  • 2 large eggs - 140kcal
  • Sauce - 48kcal
