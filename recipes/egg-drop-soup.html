Description

Egg drop soup is a classic Chinese comfort dish known for its delicate egg ribbons floating in a savory broth. Made by slowly drizzling beaten eggs into hot chicken stock, the soup thickens slightly with a touch of cornstarch and is seasoned with white pepper and a hint of sugar. It‚Äôs light, soothing, and incredibly easy to make‚Äîperfect as a starter or a quick, nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken stock (Knorr)
  • 1 egg(beaten with 1tsp water)
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch + 3/4 tsp water
  • 1 pinch white pepper
  • 1 pinch sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the Stock:

    In a small pot, bring chicken stock to a simmer. Add white pepper and sugar.

  2. Add the Cornstarch Mixture:

    Stir in the cornstarch slurry while stirring the soup gently to avoid clumps.

  3. Add the egg

    Once slightly thickened, slowly drizzle in the beaten egg while stirring in a circular motion to create egg ribbons.

  4. Finish and Serve:

    Simmer for another 30 seconds, then serve!
